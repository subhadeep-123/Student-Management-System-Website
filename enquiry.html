How to Reach

By Train: From Sealdah Station, take the Budge Budge Local & get down at Nangi Station. TI-B is about 1km from Nangi Station.

By Bus: Howrah - Batanagar Mini and S47.
             From Taratala take any bus that goes to Budge Budge .

Distance: Dharmatala to Campus: about 16 km, Taratala to Campus: about 9 km.
